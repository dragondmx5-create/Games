# The Six Lands

All names and content below are authored in `src/overworld/registry.ts`.

## 1. The Witchlands

**Capital:** Gloamrest  
**Identity:** curse fog, dead woodland, occult craft and corrupted wildlife.  
**Secondary settlements:** Fenwatch, Briar Chapel, Crow's Lantern, The Crooked Cellar.  
**Dungeons:** Hagspire Cellars, Bone Orchard.  
**Hidden route:** a cellar network beneath a witch settlement.  
**Signature pressure:** curse fog and ambush density.

## 2. Green Land

**Capital:** Evergrove  
**Identity:** farming, rivers, timber, ordinary trade and temperate wildlife.  
**Secondary settlements:** Millhaven, Rivercross, Hunter's Rest, The Rootcellar.  
**Dungeons:** Old Crown Mine, Briarhold Keep.  
**Hidden route:** a sealed mill race.  
**Signature pressure:** storms and overgrown frontier roads.

## 3. Rainforest

**Capital:** Canopy Crown  
**Identity:** vertical settlements, monsoons, flooded ruins, rare botany and venom.  
**Secondary settlements:** Orchid Step, River Teeth, Monsoon Watch, Waterfall Den.  
**Dungeons:** Sunken Temple, Serpent Observatory.  
**Hidden route:** a cavern behind a waterfall.  
**Signature pressure:** flooding, visibility and predator ambushes.

## 4. Frostlands

**Capital:** Frosthold  
**Identity:** caravans, icecraft, rescue culture and cold-adapted fauna.  
**Secondary settlements:** White Pine, Glass Lake, Aurora Post, Underice.  
**Dungeons:** Aurora Vault, Mammoth Grave.  
**Hidden route:** a tunnel beneath a frozen lake.  
**Signature pressure:** whiteouts and lethal exposure.

## 5. Sunscorched Desert

**Capital:** Solspire  
**Identity:** caravans, water law, relic trade, tombs and moving dunes.  
**Secondary settlements:** Salt Meridian, Oasis Nine, Sunward Post, Buried Cistern.  
**Dungeons:** Kingless Tomb, Glass Furnace.  
**Hidden route:** a dry cistern descending below the dunes.  
**Signature pressure:** sandstorms, heat and route loss.

## 6. Cinder Coast

**Capital:** Emberport  
**Identity:** volcanic beaches, salvage towns, wreck architecture and dangerous sea lanes.  
**Secondary settlements:** Wreckward, Smoke Tide, Caldera Watch, Dead Reckoning.  
**Dungeons:** Drowned Foundry, Ashen Lighthouse.  
**Hidden route:** a pirate-controlled wreck channel.  
**Signature pressure:** eruptions, sulfur and unstable coastlines.

## Settlement rules

Each land has:

- One public capital.
- Two public towns.
- One public outpost.
- One hidden settlement.

Settlements are functional safe hubs generated from the same world system as wilderness regions. Their final art, building layouts and service rosters will be expanded during the asset/content phase.
